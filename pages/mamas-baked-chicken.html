Mama's Baked Chicken

Return to All Recipes

This recipe is a personal favorite, and by "a personal favorite" I really mean "my personal favorite." It's the best. Buttery, crusty, and a little bit smoky, this chicken bake will burn the roof of your mouth, for better or worse!

To begin, gather the necessary ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 2 tblsp. lemon juice

  • 1/2 cup Parmesan
