Mama's Baked Chicken

Return to All Recipes

This recipe is a personal favorite, and by "a personal favorite" I really mean "my personal favorite." It's the best. Buttery, crusty, and a little bit smoky, this chicken bake will burn the roof of your mouth, for better or worse!

To begin, gather the necessary ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 2 tblsp. lemon juice
  • 2 tsp. garlic salt
  • 2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 3 tsp. chipotle powder
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan

Steps to Make

  1. If you don't have premade breadcrumbs (which is actually ideal), dry 5-6 slices of bread in your oven at 225 degrees F, then process them briskly in a food processor. Sift out smaller crumbs as needed to process the larger ones.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Do not use convection bake or you will burn the bread crumbs!
  3. Mix bread crumbs with garlic salt, garlic powder, chipotle powder, and Parmsesan.
  4. Add thawed chicken to a pyrex baking dish. Cover and stuff crannies with freshly seasoned crumbs.
  5. Melt the butter then mix with lemon juice. Drizzle across the chicken.
  6. Bake approx. 1 hour or until 175 degrees F. Cover with foil if the crumbs are blackening too quickly.
  7. Enjoy with carrots and noodles on the side!
